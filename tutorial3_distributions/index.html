Gamestop Reddit Post Comments vs. Upvotes

This scatterplot displays number of comments vs upvotes for Reddit posts about Gamestop stock. The data source can be found here: on Kaggle (Stock Market Subreddits.

The posts are categorized by three Subreddits:

  1. Investing
  2. Stocks
  3. Wallstreet Bets

I think it's interesting how many more upvotes and posts happen on Wallstreet Bets vs. the other categories.
